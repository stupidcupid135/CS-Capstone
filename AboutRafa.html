Rafa Davis (Any Pronouns)

In high school, many of my teachers refused to say it aloud. Ms. Finkel always mispronounced it as "Doofenshmirtz," and eventually it stuck! That's why I've invented the Disgustinator! Now, with its help, I will use it to create disgust across the entire tri-city area!!!
